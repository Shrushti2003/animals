Superman

Superman, also known as Clark Kent, is a fictional superhero created by writer Jerry Siegel and artist Joe Shuster. First appearing in Action Comics #1 in 1938, Superman is an iconic character in the DC Comics universe. Born on the planet Krypton, he possesses superhuman abilities such as super strength, speed, flight, and heat vision due to Earth's yellow sun.
